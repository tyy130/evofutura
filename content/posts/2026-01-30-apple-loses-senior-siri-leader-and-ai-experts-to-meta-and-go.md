title: Apple Loses Senior Siri Leader and AI Experts to Meta and Google DeepMind

Apple continues to lose key talent from its artificial intelligence and Siri teams, adding pressure to an already sensitive transition period. Several researchers and a senior Siri executive have left in recent weeks, many heading to rival AI labs. The exits follow months of internal reshuffling and come as Apple prepares major AI upgrades tied to Siri.

According to a new report from Bloomberg, the latest departures include four artificial intelligence researchers alongside a top Siri executive. The report says these employees have moved to companies such as Meta and Google DeepMind, underscoring how competitive the market for AI talent has become.

More AI Researchers Exit Apple

The researchers named in the report are Yinfei Yang, Haoxuan You, Bailin Wang, and Zirui Wang. Their next steps point in different directions, but the pattern remains clear. Apple continues to lose experienced people working on core AI systems.

"The latest exits include Yinfei Yang, Haoxuan You, Bailin Wang and Zirui Wang. Yang left to start a new company, while You and Bailin Wang joined Meta," the report says.

Haoxuan You has joined Meta's Superintelligence research arm, while Bailin Wang is now working on Meta's recommendation systems. In Zirui Wang's case, the move is to Google DeepMind, an AI lab that also plays a role in supporting Apple's own future AI features.

Senior Siri Executive Also Departs

The report also notes the departure of Stuart Bowers, described as one of Apple's most senior executives working on Siri. Bowers recently saw his responsibilities expand and reported directly to Siri's chief, Mike Rockwell. He also previously worked on Apple's now canceled self driving car project.
