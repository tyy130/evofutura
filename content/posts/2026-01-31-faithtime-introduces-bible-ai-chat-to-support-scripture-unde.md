January 30, 2026 - PRESSADVANTAGE -

FaithTime, a comprehensive faith-based mobile application, has launched its new Bible AI Chat feature, designed to provide users with immediate, conversational access to Scripture understanding and spiritual guidance. The introduction of this AI-powered tool marks a significant step in making biblical knowledge more accessible to individuals seeking to deepen their relationship with God through daily devotional practices.

The newly launched Bible AI Chat experience enables users to engage with Scripture in a dynamic, interactive format. Users can now ask questions about biblical passages, theological concepts, or spiritual matters at any point during their reading and receive clear, contextually relevant responses in real time. This conversational approach to Scripture study removes barriers that often prevent people from exploring the Bible more deeply, particularly those who may feel intimidated by complex theological language or unsure where to begin their spiritual journey.

At the core of the FaithTime bible AI chat functionality is its ability to provide verse references alongside explanations, ensuring that users can verify information directly from Scripture while gaining deeper insight into the meaning and application of God's Word. The AI assistant offers thoughtful interpretations that help bridge the gap between ancient biblical text and modern-day life, making it easier for believers to understand how scriptural principles apply to their personal circumstances and daily challenges.

"We believe that technology, when guided by compassion and purpose, can be a powerful tool for spiritual growth," said Kyle Buss, Spokesperson for FaithTime. "The Bible AI Chat feature is designed to help believers explore Scripture with confidence and clarity, making God's Word more accessible while supporting the consistency that genuine spiritual formation requires."
