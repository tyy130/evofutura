# Serverless Architecture in 2026

The pendulum has swung back. After years of Kubernetes sprawl, enterprises are realizing that managing clusters is a cost center, not a differentiator.

## Why Serverless Now?
- **Granular Billing:** Pay for execution, not idle RAM.
- **Scale-to-Zero:** Essential for non-production environments.
- **FinOps Integration:** Cloud providers now offer better visibility into function-level costs.
