Lead AI Solutions Architect on Microsoft Azure

January 30, 2026 1 min read via Head Topics

Seeking a Lead AI Solutions Architect to design, build, and deploy production-grade AI solutions on Microsoft Azure. The role encompasses end-to-end delivery, from data pipelines to model deployment, with a focus on security, reliability, and compliance. Expertise in RAG systems, prompt engineering, and Responsible AI is crucial. Experience in financial services is a plus.

The role involves spearheading the design, construction, and operational deployment of sophisticated, production-ready Artificial Intelligence solutions on the Microsoft Azure platform.

The individual will be responsible for the complete lifecycle of these solutions, spanning the creation and maintenance of data pipelines, right through to the deployment of machine learning models, always ensuring adherence to stringent security protocols, unwavering reliability, and all relevant regulatory compliance standards. This entails a deep understanding of Azure AI services and the ability to leverage them effectively. The scope of work includes the design and implementation of complex AI solutions utilizing a range of Azure AI services such as OpenAI, AI Search, and Machine Learning. The successful candidate will also be responsible for containerizing workloads, facilitating their deployment to environments like Azure Kubernetes Service (AKS), Azure App Service, or Azure Functions, and setting up and managing Continuous Integration/Continuous Deployment (CI/CD) pipelines to streamline the release process and ensure efficiency. Collaboration is a key aspect, requiring seamless teamwork with cross-functional teams comprising various stakeholders.
