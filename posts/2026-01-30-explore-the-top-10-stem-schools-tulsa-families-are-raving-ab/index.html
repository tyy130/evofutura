For families interested in specialized education, STEM (science, technology, engineering and mathematics) schools may be the next step for your learner. Many schools lean into these subjects by encouraging hands-on learning, critical thinking and real-world application.

With plenty of options across Tulsa, there’s a program to support every student’s needs.

Whether you’re raising a future engineer or the area’s next best surgeon, these schools will foster innovation and encourage advanced learning. Based on online data, community reviews, and information from sites like Niche, explore the best STEM schools in Tulsa and launch your child on a lifelong path to success.

For nearly 100 years, Holland has provided a liberal arts-focused “college preparatory curriculum that promotes critical thinking and lifelong learning.” With AP classes available for every discipline, students can choose from courses such as AP Chemistry and AP Calculus. Even staff receive higher-level learning through mentors from the University of Tulsa.

What students and parents are saying:

“The school has one of the most unique communities that you can’t find anywhere else. It is supportive, fun and exciting. There are unlimited resources for any student to succeed,” said a former student.

With an emphasis on a liberal arts education, Cascia Hall is ranked as the No.1 Best Private High School in Oklahoma, according to Niche. The institution already hosts an expansive curriculum, but is currently constructing a new state-of-the-art STEM Education wing, complete with robotics shops and college-level science labs. STEM-focused extracurriculars are also available.

What students and parents are saying:

“Cascia Hall’s campus is among the most beautiful in the country and is filled with happy students & teachers. The balance of traditional liberal arts education with STEM preparation is perhaps my favorite part about Cascia,” said a parent.
