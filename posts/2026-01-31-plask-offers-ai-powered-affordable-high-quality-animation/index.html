Investment Stage : Series A

Key Product : Gamable

Industry : AI+Media·Content

Technology : AI Model·Architecture / Generative AI

Website : plask.ai

Plask offers its previsualization tools as a SaaS solution, improving real-time collaboration through a web-based platform.

Generative AI, Virtual Humans, and Content Automation

Holding a total of 22 intellectual property rights, including domestic and international patents, trademarks, and design rights related to generative AI, Plask conducts its business in the areas of generative AI, virtual humans, and content automation.

In Korea, Plask serves indie game developers who want to make their own games, educational institutions from middle schools to universities, and organizations running creative education programs. Internationally, it operates in North America and Europe, targeting indie game creators, metaverse/user-generated content (UGC) platform users, and global game publishers.

The company actively promotes its solutions at global exhibitions such as GDC (U.S.) and IBC (Europe) through demo experience zones and hands-on workshops, while building its partner network via local reseller channels in Europe and North America and through adoption in educational curricula.

In the short term (1-2 years), it plans to stabilize its beta service and launch a creator rewards program. It also plans to increase its presence at global exhibitions.

In the medium term (3-4 years), it plans to establish North American and European reseller channels, formalize adoption in educational curricula, and host quarterly online hackathons. In the long term (5 years), it plans to expand its share of the global game development tools market and achieve annual growth of over 80% in ARR.
